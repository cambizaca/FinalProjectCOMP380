TIPS FOR INTERNET SAFETY

Even though there are risks to using social media, we understand that it is an important part in staying connected to others. So, here are some tips for using social media in the safest way possible:

  • Do not provide any sensitive information to anyone or any website without fully understanding who is asking for this personal information and why they need it.
  • Always approach phone calls asking for personal information with high skepticism.
  • Make sure your privacy settings are set to your personal specifications.
